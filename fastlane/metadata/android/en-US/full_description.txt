Vayou is a fast, free and ad-free video player for Android. It plays the videos stored on your device and on your local network, with full control over audio tracks, subtitles, playback speed and screen gestures — all in a clean Material You interface.

Whether you are watching movies, series or your own recordings, Vayou is built to handle almost any file you throw at it, with hardware-accelerated playback and wide format support.

PLAY ANY VIDEO
 * Plays local files from your device storage
 * Built-in media picker with tree, folder and file views
 * Open videos from a URL or stream
 * Hardware-accelerated playback with software decoder fallback

SUBTITLES
 * Built-in and external subtitle support (SRT, SSA, ASS, TTML, VTT)
 * Search and download subtitles from OpenSubtitles
 * Automatic subtitle translation while you watch
 * Audio and subtitle track selection

NETWORK & LIVE TV
 * Play videos from network shares (SMB)
 * Add and watch IPTV / live TV playlists
 * Organize content with playlists and favorites

GESTURES & CONTROLS
 * Swipe vertically to change brightness (left) or volume (right)
 * Swipe horizontally to seek
 * Pinch to zoom, resize modes (fit / stretch / crop / 100%)
 * Playback speed control and volume boost
 * Controls lock and picture-in-picture mode

SUPPORTED FORMATS
 * Video: H.263, H.264 AVC, H.265 HEVC, MPEG-4 SP, VP8, VP9, AV1
 * Audio: Vorbis, Opus, FLAC, ALAC, PCM/WAVE, MP1, MP2, MP3, AMR, AAC, AC-3, E-AC-3, DTS, DTS-HD, TrueHD
 * Streaming: DASH, HLS, RTSP
 * Subtitles: SRT, SSA, ASS, TTML, VTT

Vayou is completely free, open source, contains no ads and requests no unnecessary permissions.
